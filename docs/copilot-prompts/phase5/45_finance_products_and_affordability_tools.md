# Prompt 45 — Finance Products and Affordability Tools

Build the buyer-hub finance education and affordability layer.

## Goals

- help customers understand finance and protection products
- provide clear payment/affordability tools without misrepresenting final deal terms
- increase confidence and conversion into quick app and inquiry flows

## Tasks

1. Build a shopper-facing finance hub page.
2. Include at minimum:
   - payment estimator or affordability calculator
   - explanation of finance products/protection products
   - clear path into quick app / finance entry
3. Ensure finance pages connect back to specific units where relevant.
4. Add or update:
   - `/docs/ux/customer_finance_experience.md`
   - `/docs/architecture/customer_finance_surface_map.md`

## Rules

- do not represent estimates as guaranteed terms
- keep language plain and trust-building
- preserve canonical relationship to internal finance objects

## Deliverable

- finance hub/affordability tools
- finance products explainer surface
- docs for customer finance UX and architecture
