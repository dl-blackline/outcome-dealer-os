# NationalCarMart.shop Premium Brand Kit

## Core Direction
Use the attached muscle-car cinematic look as the primary visual reference:
- dark steel and black base
- wet asphalt reflections
- high-contrast lighting
- red / blue / white neon motion streak accents
- premium but aggressive dealership tone
- modern performance-driven layout, not a generic template auto site

## Brand Personality
- Powerful
- Premium
- Fast
- Trustworthy
- Cleveland-rooted
- Finance-friendly without feeling cheap

## Visual Rules
### Colors
- National Red: #E31B37
- National Blue: #1E3A8A
- Steel Silver: #C0C3C7
- Charcoal: #111418
- Dark Steel: #1B1E23
- White: #FFFFFF

### Surfaces
- brushed steel headers
- subtle hex-pattern / smoked black backgrounds
- glossy vehicle cards
- glass / steel panel feel
- thin red and blue edge glows only where needed

### Typography
- Headlines: bold, condensed, wide, high-impact
- Body copy: clean sans-serif, readable, modern
- CTA buttons: strong uppercase

## Sitewide UX Direction
### Header
- dark steel bar
- slim premium logo
- compact nav
- primary CTA button: GET APPROVED

### Homepage
1. Massive cinematic hero
2. 3 CTA row: Shop Inventory / Get Approved / Value Trade
3. 4 service cards: Inventory / Financing / Trade / Service
4. Featured inventory slider
5. Financing confidence section
6. Trust row: family-owned, fast approvals, Cleveland selection
7. premium footer

### Inventory Page
- dark filter rail
- large premium cards
- badge system: New Arrival / Great Value / Family Favorite / Premium
- red price, blue payment
- prominent Get Approved CTA on every card

### VDP
- cinematic gallery
- sticky finance / trade / availability panel
- vehicle spec strip
- financing module below gallery
- similar units carousel

### Finance Page
- performance-style hero, not stock finance art
- fast form above the fold
- “No impact to score” confidence messaging
- bad credit / no credit / first-time buyer / family vehicle lanes
- FAQ and “what to bring” section

## Content Tone
Use direct-response dealership copy:
- Drive Something Powerful
- Fast Easy Approvals
- Cleveland Confidence
- Financing For Every Drive
- Family-Owned Since 1962
- Premium Pre-Owned Vehicles

## Asset Notes
Do not make the site look overstuffed or like a flyer.
Keep:
- more space
- stronger hierarchy
- fewer but larger statements
- image-led sections
- polished hover states
